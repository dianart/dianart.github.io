I'm Diana.

I'm a first generation college junior at UC Berkeley, studying Sociology. I'm also Mexican-American and Californian so the algorithm would correctly predict that I love avocadoes. Avocadoes are my spirit fruit.

I spend my free time learning about web design and laughing and making others laugh. As one of my friends once said, "If Diana isn't laughing, something is wrong."

I am passionate about making the tech industry inclusive, especially for people of color, and I am actively involved in efforts to do so (see below). I love travel so much that I want to be digital nomad.

I do things.

I am the lead for the women of color inclusion team. I plan events and reach out to organizations.

I am an ambassador for BeVisible Latina, a social media startup that seeks to connect Latinas with each other to collaborate and network.
