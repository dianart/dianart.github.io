I do things.

FEM Tech Berkeley

I am the lead for the women of color inclusion team. My goal is to increase the representation of women of color in the club and reach out to organizations to collaborate with.

About FEM Tech: FEM Tech's goal is to increase gender inclusivity in tech and inspire people, especially women, from all majors to have their voices heard in tech, learn how to code, get mentored, and network with professionals. No tech experience is required, only a desire to learn and be part of a supportive community.

BeVisible

I am an ambassador for BeVisible Latina and I plan events to connect Latina millienials with each other. Our most recent event was Latinas In Startups.

About BeVisible: BeVisible is a career focused social media platform for US Latinas. BeVisible is about connecting Latinas with each other and to educational, career, entrepreneurial, and mentoring opportunities to give them the tools they need to succeed in education, at work, in government, and in their communities.
